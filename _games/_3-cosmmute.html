This was a small game coded by myself in Unity 3D in 48 hours. The game jam theme was public transportation, so I decided to make transit for lost cosmonauts.

Thanks to Meg Linard for some of the 2D art.

Controls: Arrow keys, pick up the cosmonaut animals on the side of your ship. Drop them off for more fuel.

Windows or Mac
