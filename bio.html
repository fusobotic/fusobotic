BIO

My name is Christian Coppoletti. I’m primarily a 3D modeler, character designer and game developer. I’ve also worked proficiently as a front-end web programmer and an illustrator. My skill set is diverse mostly because I pick up new tools quickly.

Anything related to digital art has been my passion ever since I was young. I got my first paycheck for 3D art at age thirteen. I’ve worked over 10k hours on personal and professional projects. Every client, professor, and colleague I’ve collaborated with has noted my exceptional attention to detail but also my overall organization and clarity. My aim is to work with those who care deeply about their field and know precisely how I can contribute. Recently I’ve done mostly contract work but I am always looking for a more permanent workplace to settle into.
